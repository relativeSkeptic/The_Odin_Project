Spaghetti

Recipe Picture

Spaghetti is a long, thin pasta originating from Italy, traditionally made from durum wheat semolina and water. It is a staple of Italian cuisine, often served with a variety of sauces such as marinara, meatballs, or pesto. Known for its versatility, spaghetti can be paired with both simple and complex dishes, making it a global favorite. It is typically cooked al dente, maintaining a firm texture.
